# Hero Image Setup Guide

## üì∏ How to Add Your Hero Image to the Intro Section

The hero image appears in the **center column** of the intro section on the home page. This is where you can display your beautiful jewelry photography (like the mannequin hand with rings).

---

## üöÄ Quick Steps

### **Step 1: Open Sanity Studio**

1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/studio` (if running locally)
   - OR go to [sanity.io/manage](https://sanity.io/manage) ‚Üí Your Project ‚Üí "Open Studio"

### **Step 2: Navigate to Site Settings**

1. In the left sidebar, click on **"Site Settings"**
2. If you don't see it, click **"All Documents"** and search for "Site Settings"
3. Click to open the Site Settings document

### **Step 3: Upload Hero Image**

1. Scroll down to the **"Hero Image"** field
2. Click the **"Select"** or **"Upload"** button
3. **Upload your image:**
   - Click **"Upload"** ‚Üí Select your image file
   - Recommended size: **1200x1200px** or larger (square or portrait)
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
   - Max file size: 10MB

### **Step 4: Add Alt Text (Important for SEO)**

1. After uploading, click on the image
2. In the image details panel, find **"Alternative Text"** field
3. Enter descriptive text, for example:
   - `"Elegant mannequin hand displaying two gold rings - one with rectangular gemstone, one with round brilliant-cut stone"`
   - `"Luxury jewelry display featuring gold rings on white hand"`
   - `"Hand model showcasing premium gold ring collection"`

### **Step 5: Publish**

1. Click the **"Publish"** button (top right)
2. Wait for confirmation that it's published
3. The image will appear on your website immediately!

## üìê Image Recommendations

### **Best Image Specifications:**

- **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1) or Portrait (3:4) works best
- **Dimensions:** 
  - Minimum: 800x800px
  - Recommended: 1200x1200px or 1200x1600px
  - Maximum: 2000x2000px (larger files may load slower)
- **File Format:** JPG (for photos) or PNG (for transparency)
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for best performance
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit, professional photography

### **Image Content Suggestions:**

‚úÖ **Good Hero Images:**
- Mannequin hand with jewelry
- Product close-ups
- Lifestyle shots with jewelry
- Clean, minimalist backgrounds
- Professional lighting

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Low resolution images
- Blurry or dark photos
- Images with text overlays
- Very large file sizes (>2MB)

## üîß Troubleshooting

### **Image Not Showing?**

1. **Check if published:**
   - Make sure you clicked "Publish" in Sanity Studio
   - Draft changes won't appear on the website

2. **Clear cache:**
   - Hard refresh: `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
   - Or clear browser cache

3. **Check image format:**
   - Ensure it's JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - Try re-uploading if corrupted

4. **Check file size:**
   - Very large files (>5MB) may not load
   - Compress the image before uploading

### **Image Looks Distorted?**

1. **Check aspect ratio:**
   - Square or portrait images work best
   - Very wide images may be cropped

2. **Use hotspot feature:**
   - In Sanity Studio, click on the image
   - Adjust the "hotspot" to focus on the important part
   - This ensures the best part of the image is visible

### **Image Loading Slowly?**

1. **Optimize image:**
   - Compress before uploading
   - Use tools like TinyPNG or ImageOptim
   - Target file size: 200-500KB

2. **Check internet connection:**
   - Sanity CDN should be fast
   - If slow, check your connection
